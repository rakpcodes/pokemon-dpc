Pokémon DTC

Welcome to Pokémon DTC! An app created using the PokéAPI that generates a unique Pokémon Digital Trading Card at the click of a button! Feel free to generate a few cards, you might even get a rare one! Check the 'About' menu to see what types of cards are available and the 'Legend' menu to get more info on your card! Enjoy!
