About

Welcome to Pokémon DTC, a jQuery app built using the PokéAPI that generates a Digital Trading Card. When you click on the Pokéball button, the card is populated with a random Pokémon and displays the Pokémon's type, stats and up to 2 random moves, all with data pulled from the API!

And since it's a trading card...

Each time you generate a card, it has a chance to become rare! Common cards will have a background colour that matches their type like so:

But... there are some cards that can be generated with a shiny background or with a shiny Pokémon image! Check out some examples:
